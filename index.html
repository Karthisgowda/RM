AeroGuard-EPProblem and Need
Research problem

Misinformation spreads faster than verification.

Social platforms reward engagement, novelty, and emotional intensity. Misleading posts can travel through large networks before fact-checking systems react.

Why it matters

  • Public health: vaccine hesitancy and rejection of evidence-based advice.
  • Politics: distorted voter perception, polarization, and weakened trust.
  • Disaster response: delayed corrective action during emergencies.
  • Society: institutional distrust and fragmented information communities.
Original RM theme: drivers, impacts, and mitigation strategies04
AeroGuard-EPGap and Objectives
Research gap

Reactive systems arrive too late.

  • Regulation is slow during high-volatility events.
  • Media literacy helps, but cannot stop rapid cascades alone.
  • Fact-checking labels often appear after belief and attention have shifted.
Objectives

Build proactive resilience.

  • Model virality using real-time diffusion dynamics.
  • Verify suspicious claims with multi-agent consensus.
  • Activate a circuit breaker before instability becomes viral.
  • Preserve privacy through federated learning concepts.
Gap: detection accuracy is not enough without containment velocity05
AeroGuard-EPProposed System
Four-layer architecture

AeroGuard-EP system design

01

Sentinel Layer

Decentralized agents monitor content and metadata patterns across social media streams.

02

Consensus Layer

Multiple verifier agents run a reputation-weighted truth assessment process called Sparta Alignment.

03

Throttling Layer

The circuit breaker dynamically reduces transmission velocity for suspicious or false content.

04

Inoculation Layer

Corrective and prebunking information is promoted to reduce continued belief in false claims.

Methodology focus: detection, verification, containment, correction06
AeroGuard-EPModel and Algorithm
Stochastic diffusion control

Stiffness ratio becomes the trigger signal.

sigma = |lambda max| / |lambda min|
s = beta * lambda / delta
  • If s > 1 and sigma > sigma crit, the circuit breaker activates.
Operational flow
1. Input

Collect content and metadata streams.

2. Estimate

Compute stiffness and epidemic threshold.

3. Verify

Run consensus with external retrieval.

4. Throttle

Reduce transmission rate and promote corrections.

AeroGuard-EPConclusion and Future Scope
Conclusion

AeroGuard-EP turns misinformation response into network control.

  • Detect suspicious spread early.
  • Verify with multiple AI agents.
  • Throttle unstable cascades and promote corrective information.
Limitations and future work

Next research direction

  • Optimize real-time Jacobian estimation at global scale.
  • Reduce consensus failure from persuasive but incorrect evidence.
  • Integrate user behavior signals through Intelligence of Social Things research.
  • Use zero-knowledge proofs for privacy-preserving agent reputation.
